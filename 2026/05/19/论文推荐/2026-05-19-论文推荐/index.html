摘要:

For a single trapped ion, the excitation spectrum of a narrow optical transition consists of a Doppler- and recoil-free carrier accompanied by motional sidebands, which are equally spaced by the trap secular frequency and lie under a Doppler-broadened envelope that is shifted by the photon recoil. Using a quantum-mechanical model of the excitation dynamics in linear ion chains, we find that sufficiently long chains concentrate the spectrum into the carrier. This effect enables efficient excitation of light ions at short wavelengths. It may also benefit multi-ion optical clocks and mixed-species quantum-logic spectroscopy.

We observe logarithmic scaling of trapping durations spanning from sub-millisecond timescales up to 1 second, without continuous cooling. 中文概要：在集成光子微环谐振器的倏逝场中实现了单个超冷铷原子的捕获（距离芯片表面仅 150-200 nm）。开发了一种类似 Sisyphus 冷却的单次散射装载机制，捕获时间从亚毫秒延伸到 1 秒。单原子与谐振器的协同性超过 1，实现了片上光子收集、光子反聚束和 Purcell 增强自发辐射。

推荐理由：单原子与集成光子芯片的强耦合是向量子网络和芯片级量子传感器迈出的重要一步。Dayan 组的这项工作展示了 Sisyphus 冷却式的单次装载机制，解决了片上原子捕获的效率瓶颈。对于将精密测量系统微型化有重要参考价值。

10. Universal Jaynes-Cummings Control of an Oscillator

🔗 arXiv:2605.18658

基本信息: - 作者: Jordan Huang, Ethan Kasaba, Thomas J. DiNapoli, Tanay Roy, Srivatsan Chakram - arXiv ID: 2605.18658 - 提交日期: 2026 年 5 月 18 日（1 天前） - 分类: quant-ph, physics.atom-ph - 作者数量: 5 位

摘要:

The Jaynes-Cummings (JC) interaction—the coherent exchange of excitations between a two-level system and a harmonic oscillator—is one of the fundamental interactions of quantum optics, realized across platforms such as cavity quantum electrodynamics, trapped ions, mechanical resonators, and superconducting circuits. Although JC interactions and qubit rotations form a universal gate set for oscillator control, practical implementations have not been demonstrated. Here we develop and experimentally demonstrate universal JC-based oscillator control by compiling arbitrary unitary gates into sequences of JC interactions and qubit rotations. […] We demonstrate universal qudit control and implement a single-qutrit gate set with a mean post-selected process fidelity of 96%, as well as ququart and ququint shift gates.

中文概要：首次实验实现基于 JC 相互作用的通用振子量子控制——将任意幺正门编译为 JC 相互作用和量子比特旋转的序列。在超导电路平台上实现了 qutrit 门保真度 96%，以及更高维度的 ququart 和 ququint 门操作，为跨平台的玻色子模式控制奠定了基础。

评分详情:

维度 得分 说明
相关性 7/10 明确提及 trapped ions 为实现平台之一，JC 相互作用是离子阱量子控制的基础机制，physics.atom-ph 分类
热度 8/10 5 位作者，首次实验演示通用 JC 振子控制
发布时间 10/10 1 天前发布
质量创新 9/10 首次实验实现通用 JC 振子控制的长期理论构想，96% 保真度 qutrit 门，证明了编译方法在多个玻色子模式维度下的可行性

推荐理由：JC 相互作用是离子阱量子计算和量子控制的核心机制。虽然该实验在超导电路上完成，但作者明确指出该方法可直接应用于包括离子阱在内的多种量子平台。如果你关注离子阱中的量子控制（如边带冷却、量子逻辑门），这篇的编译思路值得了解。
